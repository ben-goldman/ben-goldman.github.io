About Me

Dear Students and Parents,

Hello, my name is Benjamin Goldman. I am a kind and hardworking junior at White Plains High School. Ever since elementary school, I have loved learning and teaching math. From mind-bending puzzles to practical tools, mathematics has always amazed me. I am now a very driven and quick-learning student, currently taking BC Calculus. I tutor students in middle and high school in math as well as other topics this summer. I am very skilled at explaining complex topics, preparing students for standardized testing, and introducing the nuances and dillemas of mathematical thought. I am looking forward to a great summer of helping others!

Everyone deserves access to a tutor. Because many people may need extra help but cannot afford a seasoned teacher, I aim to provide a very affordable service to those who are struggling with academics but want to succeed. I also believe that as a high schooler who took the common core, I can connect to students of all ages and relate to the pressures of school.

Please contact me if you would like references or if you would like to learn more. I look forwarard to helping out.

Sincerely,
Ben
